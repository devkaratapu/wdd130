Overview

Purpose

With our website, you'll have access to a comprehensive database of registered players, where you can create teams, schedule matches, and track scores and statistics. Our unique selling proposition lies in our commitment to fostering camaraderie and promoting fair play. We aim to create an inclusive environment where players of all skill levels can come together to enjoy the game.

Audience

My target audience for this website is primarily BYU-Idaho students, Attention all cricket enthusiasts at BYU Idaho! Are you tired of searching for like-minded individuals to play the game you love? Look no further! Introducing the official website for the BYU Idaho Cricket group. Our goal is to bring together passionate students who share a love for cricket and provide them with a platform to connect, organize matches, and foster a thriving cricket community on campus. With our website, you'll have access to a comprehensive database of registered players, where you can create teams, schedule matches, and track scores and statistics. Our unique selling proposition lies in our commitment to fostering camaraderie and promoting fair play. We aim to create an inclusive environment where players of all skill levels can come together to enjoy the game. So, why wait? Join our website and become part of an exciting cricket community at BYU Idaho. Are you ready to swing the bat and hit it out of the park? Join us today and experience the thrill of cricket like never before!"
